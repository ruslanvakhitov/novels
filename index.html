He whispered slowly in her ear. His words tickled her ear and she giggled slightly while they both made their way to the dance floor.

The music started out slow, sensous, they circled each other, their gaze remained locked. Henry placed his hand on her back, her hand on his shoulder, and their free hands finally met. Together, they danced to the music, their feet in perfect sync.

As the song progressed, the beat got faster, Claire felt more relaxed, and allowed a small smile to form on her lips while staring at the baby blue eyes of Henry. He was great at dancing. His body seemed to move with the rhythm of the music effortlessly, he made it look flawless.

Claire watched him quietly, she liked watching him. There was always something new she discovered about him while watching him.

He turned elegantly, his body in tune with the music. Yet, there was a sort of harshness to him, like he was someone who shouldn't be underestimated. Claire didn't quite care at the moment. She was in Bora Bora! She was enjoying herself!

But she couldn't deny that something seemed to be happening between them, the warmth between them grew more powerful by the second. Her heartbeat was growing steadily along with it.

She looked away suddenly, breaking eye contact with him.

This cannot happen.

She told herself.

I cannot fall in love with him!
